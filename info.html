Information

Dave's petting zoo and farm is a family run establishment for families it was started in the early 1900's by our great,great,great-grandfather David Farmsworth. We have dedicated our lives to our farm animals and you! We take pride in our warm hospitality and our caring team. We also have a small creamery by Glasgow that was established in 1957 we have 4 mitchelin stars and have (unofficaly) the best ice cream in scotland!
